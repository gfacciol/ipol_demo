You can choose one parameter for this algorithm: r, the radius of the rotating ball. If multiple radii are used enter them as a list of radii separated by spaces (e.g. 0.1 0.2 0.3)
The proposed radius corresponds to:

  • A chosen radius or series of radii if the pointset was selected among the proposed pointsets
  • An estimation of a radius (see the paper for details)

The estimated radius might not be optimal, in that case check the output execution details on the next page and choose a more adequate radius based on the octree statistics.
